Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 May 2026

Poker Run is a GPS card collection app for participating in the moreton bay classic poker run event.

Information We Collect

The app may collect the vessel name you enter, anonymous app account identifiers, event participation details, waypoint card claims, leaderboard results, and approximate location information used to verify when you are at an event waypoint.

Location Data

The app uses your location while the app is open to determine whether you are within range of event waypoints. Location is used for card collection and event verification. The app does not request background location access.

How We Use Information

We use information to operate the poker run event, record collected cards, submit completed hands, display leaderboard results, prevent duplicate or invalid submissions, and provide support.

Data Storage

Event and app data is stored using Supabase, our backend service provider. Data may be processed and stored by Supabase according to its infrastructure and service practices.

Sharing

Leaderboard information, including vessel names and results, may be visible to other event participants. We do not sell personal information.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, contact us at mbc.pokerrun.support@gmail.com.
