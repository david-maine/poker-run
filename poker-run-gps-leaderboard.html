How the active boat view works:
  • Before you start, enter GPS latitude / longitude for each waypoint (or tap Use current when you’re sitting at that point).
  • Tap Start GPS tracking & auto-deal at the beginning of the run.
  • When you come within about 150 m of an undealt waypoint, a card will be automatically dealt.
  • Once all 5 cards are dealt, give the run a boat name and tap Save this hand to leaderboard.
  • Then tap Clear waypoints & reshuffle to prepare for the next boat.
